Hai-Jun Tian

Professor at Hangzhou Dianzi University, native of Xiangyang, Hubei Province, and recipient of the Hubei Provincial Outstanding Youth Fund. He received his Bachelor's, Master's, and Ph.D. degrees from Central China Normal University in 2004, 2007, and 2010, respectively. He visited the Johns Hopkins University (USA) in 2009, the Max Planck Institute for Astronomy (Germany) in 2016, and the University of Cambridge (UK) in 2026. He has been working at Hangzhou Dianzi University since February 2023.
