HDU
Tianwen (天问, "Heavenly Questions") is a vibrant Astronomy & Astrophysics group at Hangzhou Dianzi University. We integrate observations, simulations, and machine learning to explore the Milky Way's structure, stellar kinematics, chemical enrichment, and hierarchical stellar systems — from wide binaries and triples to the intriguing "Stellar Snake." We also develop innovative astronomical techniques, including the AI-powered spectral visualization and analysis platform SpecZoo, and pursue active research programs in radio astronomy. All driven by curiosity, and powered by code.
