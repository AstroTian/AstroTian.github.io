HDU
Tianwen (天问, “Heavenly Questions”) is an astrophysics research group at Hangzhou Dianzi University. We explore the structure and evolution of the Milky Way, stellar kinematics, binary/multiple star systems, and astronomical big-data science — combining observations, simulations, and machine learning.
